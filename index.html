AKAN NAMING
Culture

This a Akan Web application that comes from
the Ghananian culture

Akan people live in Akanland and are one of
the few matrilineal societies in west Africa.
The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their
children after the day of the week they were born
and the order in which they were born.
These "day names" have further meanings
concerning the soul and character of the person.
